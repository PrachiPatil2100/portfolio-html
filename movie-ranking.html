My favourite movies in horror

"Experience suspense, twists, and gripping storytelling in these thrilling Hindi movies."

Andhadhun (2018)

This film follows the story of a blind pianist who becomes embroiled in a series of mysterious events after witnessing a murder. The film is known for its unpredictable plot twists, dark humor, and stellar performances by Ayushmann Khurrana, Tabu, and Radhika Apte. It's a gripping thriller that keeps you on the edge of your seat until the very end.

Talaash: The Answer Lies Within (2012)

Starring Aamir Khan, Kareena Kapoor Khan, and Rani Mukerji, "Talaash" is a psychological thriller that revolves around a police officer's investigation into the death of a popular actor. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers unsettling truths about himself and the people around him. The film combines elements of suspense, drama, and supernatural intrigue, making it a compelling watch.

Drishyam (2015)

A gripping thriller that revolves around a common man played by Ajay Devgn who goes to extreme lengths to protect his family when they become embroiled in a crime. The film is praised for its tight screenplay, intense performances, and unexpected twists. It keeps the audience engaged from start to finish with its thrilling narrative and moral dilemmas.
